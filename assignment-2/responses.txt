## 1. One way data travels on the internet
Information and data travels from one IP address to another through server connections
 in packages consisting of binary code. 


## 2. What is the relationship between an IP and the DNS?
An IP address is a unique address for every device that allows for information to move
to and from your device. The DNS is a server that associates words and URLs with the appropriate IP address and allows us
to navigate the internet with ease.



## 3. What is a cookie?

A cookie remembers information like your login and password for
protected websites. This cookie in your browser recognizes you from previous visits and can make decisions
based off of that information.
