Crochet Miniture Backpack

I love crocheting gifts for people for holidays and special occasions. With holidays just around the corner I thought this would be a fun project to try and share.

  • size 2.5 mm crotchet hook
  • a key ring
  • a large bead or button
  • thread and an needle
  • marker
